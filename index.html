Dancing with Shadows in the Moonlight District

Last week, I agreed to “escort” a half-drunk noble through Silverymoon's Moonlight District. I expected a quiet night, a few silver coins, and maybe a decent bottle of elven wine. What I got instead was a rooftop chase, a cursed painting, and a very polite Shadow Demon with surprisingly good manners. (Also: I may have accidentally promised my soul in exchange for a pastry.
